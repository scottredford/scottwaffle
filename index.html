SEO Case Study

We Beat Amazon & Added 58,000 Monthly Visitors.

I implemented a comprehensive SEO strategy for eBook Eros, one of the top romance eBook stores, to combat stagnation and fierce competition from Amazon and Barnes & Noble. By optimizing over 10,000 SKUs, improving conversion rates, and executing a robust link-building campaign, we boosted monthly traffic by 58,000 visitors. The site achieved top rankings for key phrases, increased its conversion rate to 7.1%, and grew sales by 45%, with the average ticket rising from $6.25 to $8.15.
